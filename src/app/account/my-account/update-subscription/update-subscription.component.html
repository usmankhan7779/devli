Once you click Save below, you will receive a prorated credit for your current plan for the remaining days of the current billing cycle. This prorated credit will be subtracted from the new cost of the plan you are changing to and you will be charged the difference. IE - if you have 5 days left on your current plan of $199.99/month and are changing your plan to a $299.99/month plan, you will be given a credit of approximately $33 and will be charged approximately $266.
